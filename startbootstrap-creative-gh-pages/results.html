Salary

When trying to figure out how much you can spend on a mortgage do these calculations

Gross Salary = $38,000

$38,000 * %0.28 = $10,640 the 28 percent is the average amount you should spend of gross salary on housing

Then do $10,640 / 12 months = $886.67 a month

To purchase a home you usually have to put down a 20% down payment
